Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Payment:

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections and we never store your payment information on our servers.
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by visiting our "Track Order" page and entering your order number, or by clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email. You can also log into your account to view all order statuses.
Yes! You can apply discount codes at checkout by entering them in the "Promo Code" field. Codes can be found in our newsletter, promotional emails, or special offers. Please note that only one discount code can be used per order, and some restrictions may apply to certain products or sale items.
Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) and overnight shipping options are also available at checkout. International shipping times vary by destination but generally take 7-14 business days. Orders placed before 2 PM EST on weekdays are processed the same day.

Your information is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes, and we regularly update our security measures to ensure your data remains protected.
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in their original condition with tags attached. Items must be returned in their original packaging. To initiate a return, visit our Returns Center or contact customer service. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Note that shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
